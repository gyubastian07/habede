Happy Birthday, Gurl!

Sorry I never say it in a proper way—
you know me, awkward and all that jazz—
but today, let me try to say it right.

Another year older, another year wiser—
but to me, you'll always be
the sunshine on my cloudy days,
the loudest cheer in my quietest moments.

May your heart be light,
your dreams take flight,
and your soul always find
reasons to dance,
even on the toughest days.

I wish you laughter that echoes for days,
friendships that stay,
and love that grows deeper
with every step you take.

Keep shining, keep slaying,
and keep being you—
because this world’s a little brighter
with you in it.

With love, from your one and only bestie.
